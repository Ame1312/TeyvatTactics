Playstyle: Neuvillette shines as a hypercarry through repeated Charged Attacks that scale with Max HP. He thrives in teams that enable quick Hydro application and energy regeneration, especially when protected by shields or interruption resistance (C1).

  2. Apply Hydro resonance / elemental debuff using off-field supports (e.g., Furina, Kokomi, Yelan).
  3. Switch to Neuvillette:
    • Use Elemental Skill if available for particles and minor AoE.
    • Hold to Charged Attack ‚Äì unleash Hydro beam for massive damage.
    • Repeat Charged Attack 2‚Äì3 times if stamina & buffs allow.
  4. When energy is full, use Elemental Burst to heal Neuvillette & deal AoE damage.
  5. Repeat rotation after cooldowns reset.

Tip: Always stay aware of Neuvillette‚Äôs HP. His Charged Attacks drain HP slowly, but his Burst heals him. A healer or shielder in the team is highly recommended for safety and uptime.
